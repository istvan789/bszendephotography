    Engagements

    What is an engagement picture?

    Pre-wedding session. Unlike your wedding photos, engagement photos don't have to be taken quite so seriously. The aim of them is to capture your relationship naturally as you have fun and enjoy quality time. Engagement shoots are a great way to capture the love between two people. Overall, it's important to let go, enjoy yourself and try to forget the camera is there! A pre-shoot chat where the couple discusses ideas and this tipically include scouting the location, planning the poses, setting up props like sweet treats. If you have any photos you would like to take, be sure to tell me so I can facilitate giving you the best session possible.

    What i provide and...

    • Can be done in almost any location
    • You can use the photos for the save the date/ wedding invite.
    • Prints if wanted
    • Edit and Retouch

    Baby Photography

    What is a Baby photography?

    Baby photography is the general term for taking pictures of human babies, from birth to about one year of age. The main focus of a posed newborn session is your baby! Poses, props and styling are used to emphasize the tiny details of your newborn in an artistic and creative way. If you have any photos you would like to take, be sure to tell me so I can facilitate giving you the best session possible.

    Types / What i provide

    • Posed baby photography
    • Fresh 48 (first days after the baby is born)
    • Prints if wanted
    • Edit and Retouch
